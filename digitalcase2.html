AQUA191V+ FrontEnd Experience

Aqua191V+ is a Vtuber who was looking for a place to put all of his links and resources.

This page was created with technological theme, inspired by a Wordpress theme that he had previously seen.

Effects such as a splashscreen, glitching links, and a lightmode feature were also added.

Some of the glitching effects were taken from opensource codepens, and modified for use in this site.
